About MDL Doors

MDL Doors has been a trusted name in the industry since 1982, providing high-quality pre-hung steel and fiberglass entrance systems. Our mission is to offer superior quality and customer satisfaction through innovation and craftsmanship.

Our Vision

We aim to be Canada's leading door manufacturer, ensuring top-notch quality and customer-centric services.

Our Facility

We are proud to operate from our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in London, Ontario, dedicated to precision and efficiency in door production.
