New Beginnings

After a late night fire devastated the original business, the brand was to live on as MDL Door Systems. Today we continue to promote MDL’s original core values.

Our highly qualified staff share our passion for precise workmanship and attention to detail. All of our components are sourced from top quality manufacturers assembled to ensure exceptional fit. Known for our custom door capabilities and quick shipping times, MDL Door Systems will satisfy your needs.

MDL Door Systems is considered one of the leaders in the entry door system industry. MDL does have a long standing reputation for building quality products with innovative design processes. Shipping times have always been top priority for our customers, and MDL Door Systems doesn’t disappoint with our Quick Ship program.

About MDL Doors

MDL Doors has been a trusted name in the industry since 1982, providing high-quality pre-hung steel and fiberglass entrance systems. Our mission is to offer superior quality and customer satisfaction through innovation and craftsmanship.

At MDL Doors, we specialize in the mass production of high-quality doors designed to meet the needs of businesses and large-scale projects. With decades of expertise in the industry, we take pride in manufacturing durable and stylish doors that enhance both functionality and aesthetics.

Our product range includes a wide variety of doors, from single doors and double doors to terrace doors, all crafted with precision and superior craftsmanship. To ensure our customers get the perfect fit for their needs, we offer extensive customization options, including glass inserts, embossments, transoms, sidelites, hinges, and more.

With a commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, MDL Doors continues to be a trusted name in the door manufacturing industry, delivering reliable solutions tailored to your specifications.

Our Vision

At MDL Doors, our vision is to become Canada’s leading manufacturer of high-quality doors, setting new standards in innovation, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to delivering exceptional products that enhance the beauty, security, and functionality of homes and businesses.

Through continuous improvement, state-of-the-art technology, and sustainable practices, we strive to exceed customer expectations and build long-lasting relationships. Our goal is to provide a diverse selection of premium doors while also offering customization options to ensure that every client finds the perfect fit for their unique needs.

Our Facility

We are proud to operate from our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in London, Ontario, dedicated to precision and efficiency in door production. We offer a wide variety of doors, including fiberglass and steel doors, designed to meet different aesthetic and functional needs.

Additionally, we provide the flexibility for customers to create custom-made doors, ensuring that every design requirement is met with superior craftsmanship and quality.
